‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is CleanFinding Browser really free?

Yes! CleanFinding Browser is completely free to download and use. There are no ads, no subscriptions, and no hidden costs. We believe privacy should be accessible to everyone.

How does CleanFinding Browser protect my privacy?

CleanFinding Browser uses multiple layers of protection: network-level tracker blocking, automatic cookie consent declining, Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals, and privacy-enhanced features like Duck Player for YouTube. We block tracking before it happens and never collect your browsing data.

What is Duck Player?

Duck Player is our YouTube privacy feature that redirects videos to youtube-nocookie.com, removes tracking parameters, blocks recommendations, and prevents Google from tracking your viewing habits. You can watch YouTube videos privately without being tracked.

Does CleanFinding Browser work on all platforms?

Yes! CleanFinding Browser is available for Android (8.0+), Windows (10/11 64-bit), macOS (10.13+), and Linux (Ubuntu 18.04+). We're working on iOS support as well.

Can I import my bookmarks from other browsers?

Currently, bookmark import is in development. For now, you can manually add your favorite sites. We're working on automatic import from Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Is CleanFinding Browser safe for children?

Yes! CleanFinding Browser includes family-safe browsing features with content filtering and safe search. We block inappropriate content and malicious websites to create a safer browsing experience for all ages.

How do I enable biometric lock?

On Android, go to Settings > Security > Biometric Lock and toggle it on. Your device must support fingerprint or face recognition. Desktop biometric support (Windows Hello, Touch ID) is coming soon.

Does blocking trackers make websites load faster?

Yes! Blocking trackers, ads, and third-party scripts significantly reduces the amount of data loaded per page. This makes websites load faster, uses less bandwidth, and saves battery life on mobile devices.

Can I disable specific privacy features?

Absolutely! You have full control over all privacy features. Go to Settings to enable or disable tracker blocking, Duck Player, email protection, cookie auto-decline, and other features individually.

How often is CleanFinding Browser updated?

We release updates regularly with new features, bug fixes, and security improvements. Major updates (like 1.4.0) come every few months, with minor updates and patches as needed.

Where can I report bugs or request features?

Visit our GitHub repository at github.com/MyRechargeHub1/cleanfinding-browser to report issues or request features. You can also contact us at support@cleanfinding.com.

Is my browsing history stored anywhere?

Your browsing history is stored locally on your device only. We never send your history to our servers or third parties. You can clear your history at any time from Settings > Clear Browsing Data. In incognito mode, no history is saved at all.
